About us

Meet the Founders

Maria, Luca, and Giovanni are not just the founders of "Taste of Italy", they are the heart and soul of the restaurant. Each of them brings their unique culinary skills and passion for Italian cuisine to the table, creating a dining experience that is truly one-of-a-kind.

Our Specialties

From Luca's hand-tossed pizzas to Maria's homemade pastas and Giovanni's decadent desserts, every dish at "Taste of Italy" is a celebration of Italian culinary traditions. Our menu is a testament to our commitment to authenticity and quality.

Experience "Taste of Italy"

At "Taste of Italy", we believe in creating a dining experience that goes beyond just good food. Our cozy trattoria is a place where you can feel at home, where you can gather with friends and family to create cherished memories. Come join us and experience the warmth of Italian hospitality.
